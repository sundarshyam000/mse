Instructions

Welcome to the Maharashtra State Council of Examination, Pune website, your one-stop destination for all examination-related services.
We are committed to providing a seamless and secure experience for our users. Please follow these instructions to make the most of our platform:


  • If you are a new user, click the "Signup" button located on the homepage. This will take you to the registration page.
  • Fill in the required information accurately to create your account. This information will include personal details, contact information, and security credentials.
  • Make sure to select a strong and secure password. Keep your password confidential and never share it with anyone. We will never ask for your password via email or other means.
  • After completing the registration form, review the provided details to ensure accuracy.
  • Once you've reviewed your information, click the "Submit" or "Register" button to create your account.
Login Instructions
  • If you already have an account, click the "Login" button on the homepage. This will take you to the login page.
  • Enter your User ID and password in the designated fields. Your User ID is auto-generated during the signup process.
  • Double-check that you've entered your credentials correctly to avoid login issues.
  • Click the "Login" button to access your account.

  • After successfully logging in or creating an account, you will be redirected to the homepage.
  • Utilize the navigation menu at the top of the page to explore various sections, including "Home," "About Us," and more.

    • **Security and Privacy:**

  • Ensure the security of your account by not sharing your password with anyone. We take your privacy seriously and will never ask for your password via email or any other communication.

    • **Contact and Support:**

  • If you encounter any issues or have questions while using our platform, our support team is here to assist you. Contact us through the provided channels, which can be found in the footer of this page.
